Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

A simple scrambled eggs recipe thats quick and delicious

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • butter
  • seasoning (optional)

Steps

  1. Put butter in pan and set heat to low
  2. While butter is melting crack eggs and into a bowl and mix them
  3. When the butter is melted and starting to brown pour the eggs into the pan
  4. Keep moving the eggs while they cook (this is when you would add your seasonings)
  5. When eggs are about half or 75% done turn off the heat
  6. Keep stirring the eggs until they reach your desired doneness
  7. Plate your eggs and enjoy!

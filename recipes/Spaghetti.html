Spaghetti

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe for spaghetti that tastes great!

Ingredients

    Butter
    Spaghetti noodles
    Pasta sauce
    Seasoning (optional)
    Cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in water on high heat, add butter
  2. When spaghetti is soft turn lower the heat, drain the noodles, and return the pot or pan to the stove
  3. Add butter to pot and let melt while spaghetti drains
  4. When spaghetti is mostly dry, return to pot and add your pasta sauce (if you want to add seasonings and/or cheese this would be time)
  5. Turn off the heat when sauce is warm
  6. Congrats, now your spaghetti is ready to serve!

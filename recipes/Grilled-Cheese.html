Grilled Cheese

Description

A quick and easy grilled cheese sandwich recipe that takesl less than 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 2 slices of bread
  • butter

Steps

  1. Turn on heat and put butter in pan
  2. Put slice of cheese between bread and press them together
  3. Place sandwich in pan and quickly flip it over to get an even coating of butter
  4. Let sandwich cook on until desired texture then flip
  5. Once again let it cook until it reaches your desired texture
  6. Turn off the heat, and enjoy your grilled cheese sandwich!
